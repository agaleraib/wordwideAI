05 // Compliance

Regulation
Built In

The compliance agent reviews every report against jurisdiction-specific rules before any human sees it. It flags issues and rewrites non-compliant sections automatically.

  • MiFID II — EU: Research independence, risk warnings
  • SEC / FINRA — US: Anti-fraud, FINRA 2210
  • FCA — UK: Fair, clear, not misleading
  • ASIC — Australia: RG 234, DDO
  • MAS — Singapore: FAA, SFA
Quality Agent → Compliance
"Report approved: EUR/USD cautiously bullish. Sending for MiFID II compliance review."
