11 // Compliance

Regulation
Built In

Compliance is a pipeline stage, not an afterthought. Every piece is scored and auditable. Every document has a complete audit trail from source to publication.

  • MiFID II — EU: Research independence, risk warnings
  • SEC / FINRA — US: Anti-fraud, FINRA 2210
  • FCA — UK: Fair, clear, not misleading
  • ASIC — Australia: RG 234, DDO
  • MAS — Singapore: FAA, SFA

What Gets Checked

  • Risk disclaimers present and correctly translated
  • No guaranteed return language ("will reach" → "may reach")
  • Past performance notices per jurisdiction
  • Forbidden terms flagged and replaced

Audit Trail

  • Source document → every transformation logged
  • Quality scores at each pipeline stage
  • Human approvals timestamped and recorded
  • Compliance certificate per document
